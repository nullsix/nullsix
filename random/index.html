The URL is the roll: /random/#2d6+3 parses the
      hash as dice notation and rolls it on load. Share a link, share a roll.
      Space rerolls. And you can pick your entropy: your browser, Cloudflare's
      lava-lamp wall, random.org's atmospheric noise, or the quantum vacuum at
      ANU — every roll comes with a
      receipt.
      (None of these make the dice fairer — your browser's randomness is
      already statistically perfect. Picking the lava lamps or the vacuum
      doesn't change the odds; it changes the story your roll tells.)
